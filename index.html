    1. Steps to Open an Online Account with SinoPac

    • Step 1: Download the App: Search for "easy by Bank SinoPac" on the App Store or Google Play, install, and open the app.
    • Step 2: Start the Registration Process: Select "Register Online Account" and click "Apply".
    • Step 3: Enter Personal Information: Provide necessary details. Auto-fill previous registration data if available.
    • Step 4: ID Verification and OTP Confirmation: Photograph your ID, enable NFC, complete eKYC, and enter OTP.
    • Step 5: Complete Setup: Review details, set password, and finish registration.

    2. Steps to Apply for an Online Loan with SinoPac

    • Step 1: Loan Application via App: Log in to the "easy by Bank SinoPac" app and select "Online Loan Application".
    • Step 2: Review Loan Details: Check the interest rate, loan limit, and tenor.
    • Step 3: Enter Loan Details: Choose amount and tenor, provide ID and mobile.
    • Step 4: Agree to Terms and Verify OTP: Review terms, agree, and enter OTP.
    • Step 5: Provide Additional Information: Enter employment details and upload required documents.
    • Step 6: Confirm and Submit Application: Review details and submit application.

    3. Steps for Loan Disbursement and Contract Signing

    • Step 1: Receive Loan Approval Notification: SMS confirmation of approval.
    • Step 2: Register for a Digital Certificate: Complete the Certificate Authority process.
    • Step 3: Sign the Loan Contract: Log in, review, and digitally sign the contract.
    • Step 4: Loan Disbursement: SMS and email confirm disbursement post-signing.
