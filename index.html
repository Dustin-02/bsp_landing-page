Frequently asked general questions

What is an Online Personal Loan?

It’s an unsecured loan offered through the Easy App by Bank SinoPac, following the bank’s policies and Vietnamese banking regulations.

What documents do I need for the loan application?

Valid ID, active mobile phone number, and financial documents (e.g., salary statements from the last 3 months, labor contract, etc.)

What is the maximum loan amount I can apply for?

You can borrow up to 500 million VND per loan.

Do I need a guarantor or collateral?

No, this is an unsecured loan, so no guarantor or collateral is required.

How is the monthly repayment calculated, and how do I pay?

Equal monthly installments based on the reducing balance. Payments are automatically deducted from your demand account each month.

What is the maximum loan duration?

You can select a loan tenor of up to 48 months.

What happens if I miss a payment?

Late payments are reported to the Credit Information Center (CIC). Additionally, overdue loans may incur additional interest based on the credit agreement.

How will the bank remind me to make repayments?

SMS reminders will be sent before your repayment is due. Please ensure you have enough funds in your SinoPac account before the due date. If you are paying through another bank, transfers made after 4 PM or on non-working days are processed the next business day. It’s recommended to transfer 1-2 days earlier to avoid delays.

When will I receive the loan approval notification?

Bank SinoPac will send an SMS immediately after your loan is approved.

Can I apply for additional loans if I already have one?

Yes, you can apply for more loans based on your financial needs.

What if I disagree with the loan terms?

You can reject the loan offer via the app or contact Bank SinoPac’s HCMC branch for assistance.

Do I need to visit a branch to sign the loan contract?

No, you can sign the contract online via the Easy App.

Why do I need a digital certificate?

A digital certificate is required to sign the loan contract online securely.

Can I use the digital certificate for other purposes?

No, the digital certificate is valid for 24 hours and can only be used to sign unsecured loan contracts with Bank SinoPac.

What if I change my mind after rejecting the loan offer?

A bank representative will contact you to confirm your decision. If you wish to proceed, you can inform them to reactivate the loan.

What if I don’t open a demand account within 7 days after agreeing to the loan?

If you don’t open the account within 7 days, your loan will be canceled, and you’ll need to apply again.

How will I receive the loan contract?

Bank SinoPac will send the contract to your registered email. The file will be encrypted with a password (your ID number).

Loan Application

  • 1 Download and Register

    Download the Easy App, register with personal information, and verify your identity.
  • 2 Complete Account Setup

    Review information, and set password.
  • 3 Access Loan Application

    Login to the Easy app, and select "Online Loan Application."

  • 4 Review Loan Details

    Check interest rate, amount, and tenor.
  • 5 Provide Loan Details

    Choose the amount, and tenor, verify identity, and agree to terms.
  • 6 Submit Application

    Provide additional info and documents, and submit an application.

Loan Disbursement

  • 1Receive Approval Notification

    Get an SMS with a contract link.
  • 2 Register for Digital Certificate

    Register a CA, verify your ID, and complete a video.
  • 3 Sign Loan Contract

    Log in to the Easy App, review the contract, and sign with your PIN.
  • 4 Loan Disbursement

    Receive confirmation of disbursement via registered SMS and email.
