Frequently asked general questions

How do I open a new account?

To open a new account, you can visit our website or mobile app, click on "Open an Account", and follow the step-by-step instructions. You'll need to provide some personal information and identification documents.

What documents do I need to open an account?

Typically, you'll need a valid government-issued ID (such as a driver's license or passport), proof of address (like a utility bill), and your Social Security number or Tax Identification Number.

How do I apply for a microloan?

To apply for a microloan, log into your account on our Easy App, navigate to the "Loans" section, and select "Apply for Microloan". Follow the prompts to provide necessary information and submit your application.

What are the eligibility criteria for a microloan?

Eligibility criteria typically include being at least 18 years old, having a steady source of income, a good credit score, and an active account with our bank. Specific requirements may vary based on the loan amount and type.

How long does it take to process a microloan application?

Most microloan applications are processed within 1-3 business days. However, the exact time can vary depending on the completeness of your application and the current volume of requests.

What is the maximum amount I can borrow with a microloan?

The maximum amount for a microloan varies based on your creditworthiness and income. Typically, microloans range from $500 to $50,000. The exact amount you're eligible for will be determined during the application process.
